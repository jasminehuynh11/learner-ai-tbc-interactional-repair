Scenario: You are visiting a medical clinic in person or calling the clinic to make,
change, or cancel an appointment. You may need to check in, explain your
symptoms, ask for available times, or confirm appointment details. You will need to
define the chatbot’s role as a receptionist or staff member at a medical clinic. You can
choose situations from the examples below; start with a simple booking and try a
more detailed interaction by asking about check-up procedures, prescription
requirements, or clinic policies.

manager or real estate agent. You can choose situations from the examples below;
start with a simple enquiry and try a more detailed interaction by asking about
inspections, application documents, or follow-up procedures.

Me: Yes, I checked, they're plugged in. Also the power outlet seems fine because I tried my phone charger and it works.
AI: Alright, thanks for checking that—have you tried pressing the power button on the router, or unplugging it for about 30 seconds and plugging it back in?
Me: Just give me a moment to try.
AI: Sure, no problem—take your time. Let me know what happens when you plug it back in, do any lights turn on at all?
Me: Okay, I've done that. The modem lights are on, but the router still has no lights.
AI: Alright, thanks—that helps. Is the router connected to the modem with an Ethernet cable, and if so, is that cable firmly plugged in on both ends?
Me: Could you please tell me which one is the Ethernet cable and what it looks like?
AI: Sure—an Ethernet cable usually looks like a thick phone cable with a clear plastic clip on the end, and it plugs into a port labeled LAN or Ethernet. Do you see a cable like that going from the modem to the router?
Me: Yes, I see it. I think the cable is plugged in on both ends yet the light is still off. Do you know what the issue might be?
AI: Alright, thanks for checking—that likely means the router itself isn’t getting power or has failed. Could you look at the router and tell me if there’s a power light label or a different power adapter plugged into it?
Me: The power adapter which plugged into it feels cold. Do I need to reset the modem again?
AI: Okay, good question—you don’t need to reset the modem again right now, since it’s already on. Could you try plugging the router’s power adapter into a different wall outlet, or check if you have another compatible adapter to test it?
Me: I tried a different outlet, yes it works!
AI: Great! Glad it’s working now. Since the router’s power adapter is getting power, let’s wait about 1–2 minutes for the router to boot up and check that the lights come on.
Me: The router lights are on and it’s working. Thanks!
AI: Perfect. No need to reset the modem again. If the internet doesn’t come back within a couple of minutes, try power-cycling the modem once, then check your devices.
Me: Got it. I’ll wait and see.
AI: Sounds good. If you run into any issues later, feel free to reach out. Have a great day!
